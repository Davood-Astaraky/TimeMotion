Analysis of length of activities

1) Distribution of the length of all tasks


Graph below presents the distribution of the length of activities after excluding erroneous outliers. It can be seen that the length of most activities involve significant number of outliers that are standing very far from other data.

Since median will not be affected by outliers, it serves as a good measure for comparison in skewed data and data full of outliers. This version of the same graph on the right has been rescaled on the length axis to better demonstrate comparison based on the median.
